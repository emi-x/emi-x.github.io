emi___x Chat Commands
!profile Links to emi's osu! profile
!latinum Displays the users total Latinum

Can be used alone to display your own total, or display other users total with syntax: !latinum [name]
!top Displays the 5 users with the highest Latinum totals
!gamble Gamble your Latinum

Required syntax: !gamble [amount]
Valid inputs for [amount] can be a whole number, a %, [amount]K, or [amount]M

Special Gamble
You have the option to enter 'SOUL' for the [amount]
This will trigger a special gamble. Winning will reward you with double your total Latinum. Losing will take all of your Latinum and give you a 30 minute timeout in chat.
!gamblestats Returns stats related to previous gambles.

Can be used alone to display your own stats, or display other users stats with syntax: !gamblestats [name]
!raffle Starts a raffle.

Required syntax: !raffle [amount]
This command is only available to users with a Security Clearance of Level 5 or higher.
!clearance Displays the users Security Clearance Level.
!commands Links you to this page
!scramble Returns a random scramble for a Rubik's Cube

Syntax: !scramble [CubeSize]
Currently supports [CubeSize] of 2, 3, 4, and 5
More commands are being developed and added. If you have any suggestions please tell me!
